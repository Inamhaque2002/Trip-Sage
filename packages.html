A: India offers a wide spectrum of experiences and landscapes. People travel in India for three main purposes: Business, Leisure, and Religious. Popular business cities include Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Ahmedabad, Kolkata,etc. Leisure travel destinations include Goa, Jaipur, Manali,etc. Religious travel destinations include Katra, Shirdi, Amritsar,etc.
